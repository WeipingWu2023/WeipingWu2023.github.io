How I stopped feeling lonely FOREVER

Created2026-04-17|Updated2026-04-17|英语乐园
|Post Views:

Summary

Link Text

This video explains that loneliness isn’t just about being alone; it happens when there is a gap between the relationships you want and the ones you actually have. Even people with many followers or friends can feel lonely if those connections aren’t meaningful.

To overcome chronic loneliness, the video suggests three main shifts:

  • Build a relationship with yourself: You cannot expect others to like you if you don’t like yourself or even know who you are. The first step is to prioritize your own needs and figure out what makes you happy—like hobbies or solo dates—without waiting for someone else to join you.
  • Stop waiting and take action: Loneliness can create a “victim mentality” where you feel resentful that others aren’t reaching out to you. You have to be the one to take initiative and text or invite people first. Reaching out is like a muscle that gets stronger the more you practice it.
  • Find a purpose outside of people: Having a goal or passion that is just for you—like starting a project or a hobby—gives you a reason to wake up that doesn’t depend on a text back from someone else. This shifts your focus from “who is thinking of me” to “what impact can I make today”.

Ultimately, loneliness is described as a wake-up call to build a deeper connection with yourself. Once you have a meaningful relationship with yourself, you will find it easier to build them with others.
